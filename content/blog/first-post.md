# Masterton Roofing: Your Experts in PVC Membrane

Masterton Roofing Limited specializes in a variety of roofing solutions, including **PVC Membrane** systems. With over 30 years of experience, we've seen why this material is a top choice for both commercial and residential properties.

## What is PVC Membrane?

Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is a single-ply white membrane used in commercial and residential roofing. Because the membrane is white, it reflects sunlight instead of absorbing it.

### Key Benefits:
- **Durability**: PVC is exceptionally strong and resistant to moisture and punctures.
- **Longevity**: With proper installation, a PVC roof can last 20-30 years.
- **Eco-Friendly**: It's often recyclable and energy-efficient due to its reflective properties.
